IGN

Intelligent Growth Network

What we are

IGN is a network of community leaders across London. We come together to share resources, identify funding opportunities and support each other to grow. No hierarchy. No gatekeeping. Just communities working together.

What we believe

Communities should not have to compete for resources. When they collaborate, everyone benefits. IGN exists to make that collaboration possible.

How it works

Community leaders join the network. We share funding opportunities, build joint proposals and distribute resources fairly based on contribution. Every leader who delivers work through IGN gets paid.

Who is it for

Anyone who runs a community, leads a group or has skills and networks that could benefit others in London. Chess clubs, yoga communities, sports programmes, language exchanges, neighbourhood networks — all welcome.
